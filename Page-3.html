Duke of Oz

Duke of Oz

The Duke of Oz in Leiden offers a vibrant, social atmosphere as a bustling sports bar. Upon entering, you'll be greeted by flat-screen TVs positioned around the bar, showcasing every sport imaginable simultaneously. While sports enthusiasts will find plenty to chat about, the bar's welcoming environment ensures that even those less interested in sports can enjoy their time here. The Australian-themed decor creates a lively and inclusive ambiance, making it a great spot for locals and international visitors alike. With a variety of delicious food options and an extensive drink menu, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Regular events, themed nights, and the friendly staff add to the dynamic experience, ensuring that whether you're a sports fan or just looking for a fun place to hang out, you'll feel right at home at The Duke of Oz.

Einstein's

Einstein's

Einstein in Leiden is a vibrant restaurant offering a friendly and social atmosphere. Located in the charming city centre, it features a big terrace where guests can enjoy their meals while overlooking the old town hall of the city. The restaurant caters to a diverse crowd, from locals to tourists, who enjoy a relaxed experience with a welcoming vibe. Einstein's menu focuses on a blend of international and local dishes. Whether you're catching up with friends or just enjoying a pleasant evening out, Einstein has a welcoming space and a satisfactory dining experience.
