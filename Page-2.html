Featured Restaurants

Tabú

Tabú Bar/Restaurant

Tabú in Leiden offers a unique dining experience with a focus on Latin American cuisine. Located just 5 minutes away from popular tourist attractions like the Leiden Hortus Botanicus and the iconic Rembrandt statue, it’s a convenient spot to visit. Upon entering, you’ll be welcomed by the friendly staff and will immediately notice the rustic and somewhat different decorations. The menu features a diverse selection of dishes, blending international flavors with Latin American favorites. Tabú aims to please diners with a fun and adventurous approach to food. The restaurant boasts a lovely view of the water and offers a charming patio where you can enjoy your meal. Definitely worth checking out!

Paco-Ciao

Paco-Ciao

Paco Ciao, located near Leiden Central Station and several popular tourist attractions, offers a charming dining experience in a beautifully restored monumental building known as the Crown Villa. The restaurant's interior design blends bohemian chic with classic elegance, reminiscent of a vintage Latin American courtyard or an aristocratic Mexican hacienda. The decor features a mix of vintage chairs, marble tables, lush tropical plants, and brass accents, all in warm earth tones. For a little surprise, I recommend you press the red button in the toilets area. With numerous cozy nooks and stunning details, Paco Ciao is visually stunning, making for great Instagram photos and stories. The menu caters to all tastes with a range of healthy options and not-so-healthy options, ensuring there’s something for everyone.
